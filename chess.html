Chess.com is one of the world's largest and most popular online chess platforms. It serves as an internet chess server, a social networking website, and an educational resource for millions of chess players around the globe. The site was re-launched in 2007 and has since grown to become a dominant force in the online chess community. ### Key Features and Services Chess.com operates on a freemium model, meaning it offers a wide range of free features, with additional, more advanced features available through a premium subscription. **For all users (free and premium), the platform provides:** * **Online Play:** Users can play live chess against other players from all over the world with various time controls, including daily, rapid, blitz, and bullet games. * **Tactics and Puzzles:** A vast database of puzzles is available to help players improve their tactical skills. * **Chess News and Articles:** The site provides extensive coverage of major chess events, tournaments, and news, often with analysis from top players and grandmasters. * **Lessons and Videos:** A selection of interactive lessons and videos are available, covering everything from the basics of how to play to advanced strategies. * **Community:** Users can join clubs, interact in forums, and connect with other players. **Premium Membership Features:** Premium memberships (Gold, Platinum, and Diamond) unlock a host of additional benefits designed to help players improve and get the most out of the platform. These include: * **Unlimited Game Review and Analysis:** A powerful feature that analyzes your games and provides move-by-move feedback, classifying moves as brilliant, excellent, good, mistakes, or blunders. It also gives an overall accuracy score. * **Unlimited Puzzles:** While free users get a limited number of puzzles, premium members have unlimited access to the entire puzzle database. * **Unlimited Lessons:** Full access to the extensive library of interactive lessons and courses. * **No Ads:** A completely ad-free experience. * **Insights:** Detailed data and statistics about a player's performance, strengths, and weaknesses. ### Impact and Influence Chess.com has played a significant role in the recent surge in chess popularity, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic. Its user-friendly interface, educational resources, and integration with live streaming platforms like Twitch have attracted a new generation of players. The platform has also hosted a number of high-profile online tournaments and events, featuring top grandmasters and popular content creators, further cementing its position as a central hub for the chess world.
